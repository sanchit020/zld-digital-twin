# Engineering Equations

## Pump Power

Pump Power

P = (Q × ΔP) / η

where

- Q = Flow Rate
- ΔP = Pressure Difference
- η = Pump Efficiency

---

## Net Power

Net Power

Net Power = Pump Power − PX Savings

---

## RO Recovery

Recovery (%) = (Permeate Flow / Feed Flow) × 100

---

## Brine Flow

Brine Flow = Feed Flow − Permeate Flow

---

## PX Savings

PX Savings = Pump Power × PX Efficiency

---

## Pump Wear

Pump Wear = Wear Rate × Operating Time

---

## Fouling

Fouling = Fouling Rate × Operating Time

---

## Water Balance

Feed Flow = Permeate Flow + Brine Flow
